Unless created by a highly skilled photographer with a keen eye for composition and lighting, static images can often be just that. Static. 360 panos on the other hand, even if not particularly well created, are at least interactive. The map shows various locations where I've taken a series of drone and land based 360 degree panos. As of December 2025 all of the land based panos were shot with a 2021 Motorola Moto G Stylus cell phone using the "Sphere" option in Gcam. The drone based panos were shot with a DJI Mini 3. Because of the relatively low 12 megapixel lens on the drone, it's possible to pull only so much detail from it. Ultimately, I'll be upgrading to possibly a DJI Mini 5 pro with a 50 megapixel lens which should provide a far greater level of detail in the images. For now, here's what I've captured.
